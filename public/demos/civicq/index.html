Why It Matters

Civic information is everywhere. Verified civic information is rare.

Most sources covering voting records, bill activity, and public statements are built to persuade first and inform second. Headlines get rewritten for outrage, votes get summarized out of context, and the original document gets lost three links deep, if it's linked at all.

CivicQ starts from the official record, official source, every time, and builds outward. Every figure on this site links back to where it came from. Every score is calculated by a published, public method. If we get something wrong, we want to hear about it, and we'll show you exactly what changed.
