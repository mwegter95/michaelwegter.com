OUR APPROACH

Therapy that fits real life, not the other way around.

Family-first

Parents are partners on every plan. We listen, we teach, and we adjust together.

Skill-building, not deficit-framing

We celebrate progress. Sessions focus on communication, daily living, and connection.

Naturalistic and play-based

Children learn best when they are engaged. Our sessions feel like play, with a plan behind every moment.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Our team comes to you. We work alongside your child in the spaces where they live, learn, and play. Sessions often include sibling and caregiver coaching so progress carries through the week.

  • 1:1 sessions in your home, typically 2 to 6 hours per visit
  • Parent coaching woven into every session
  • Weekly BCBA review and plan adjustments
Request in-home therapy

WHAT TO EXPECT

A purpose-built space for focused learning and joyful social practice. Children rotate through 1:1 sessions, peer play, and snack and circle time, with predictable routines that build comfort fast.

  • Half-day and full-day options
  • Structured social opportunities with peers
  • Bright, sensory-aware classrooms
Request center-based services

WHAT TO EXPECT

You are your child's most important teacher. Our parent training program gives you the language, strategies, and confidence to support communication, routines, and connection at home.

  • Weekly virtual or in-person sessions with a BCBA
  • Practical strategies you can use the same day
  • Optional sibling and grandparent sessions
Request parent training

WHAT TO EXPECT

We partner with teachers, IEP teams, and school staff so the supports your child has at home travel with them through the school day. Observations, coaching, and clear written plans.

  • Classroom observations and recommendations
  • IEP and 504 plan support
  • Teacher and aide coaching
